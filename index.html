Bacterial Biodiversity in the Navel

In an effort to find a type of bacteria that can be used to create a product that tastes similar to beef, we have been exploring bacteria in the navel. This is an analysis of different types of bacteria that exist within the navel for a number of participants in the study. This shows the amount of and type of bacteria found in the navel of each participant as well as how often the particpant cleaned their bellybutton.
